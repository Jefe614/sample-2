This my favourite meal

Ingridients:

  1. ...1 teaspoon Cloves.
  2. ...2 tbsp Cumin seeds.
  3. ...1/2 tbsp black pepper seeds.
  4. ...12 pods cardamon.
  5. ...2 cups basmat Rice.
  6. ...5 potatoes
  7. ...red onions
  8. ...beef cubes
  9. ...salt

Instructions

Make pilau masala:

  1. ...place the spices on a dry pan
  2. ...roast over low heat until warmed
  3. ...bra bra bra

Make the rice pilau:

  1. ...fry the onions about 5 min
  2. ...add cubed beef meat and pilau masala, cook until meat browns
  3. ...add the deced tomatoes cook for 5 min
  4. ...stir in the potatoes add water cook for about 20min
  5. ...bra bra bra
  6. ...serve when hot
